Programming

Compensation Social

Developed by Subsurface Studios

Avery started her programming career with a video game project known as Compensation Social. Compensation Social was a VR Multiplayer game made in the Unity game engine by Avery and her friend Rose. The project featured numerous built-in minigames such as a climbing game known as "A Helpful Hand", an Airsoft PVP game with two arenas, and many lounges to hang around in. Unfortunately, Compensation Social's development ended in 2023 due to lack of player interest and the developer's lack of desire to sustain the project.

BYTE

Developed by Subsurface Studios

BYTE was another game project developed by Subsurface Studios. BYTE was to be a first person shooter akin to ULTRAKILL, it was mainly developed in Unity, before momentarily switching to Unreal Engine 5. The project's first OST release can still be found on Bandcamp. Despite numerous attempts, development on BYTE has not been completed.
